About us

Whether your organization requires a stand alone or integrated security solution, which can be monitored from any location, I-Witness is the right choice. Our solutions incorporate some of the most advanced technologies ranging from, Network IP Cameras to Iris & Fingerprint recognition systems. We also provide a range of conventional systems such as standard CCTV systems and Proximity card access control designed to meet the needs of all organizations and businesses.

CCTV is the core of every security system and remains the ideal method for remotely monitoring access points and detecting unauthorized acts. CCTV is the most powerful tool to monitor activities and deter crime. No security system designed to protect the premises of an organization or business is complete without effective CCTV system. Allowing multiple views of internal and external locations to be both monitored and stored, CCTV systems provide an accurate account of both past and present events. This is a cost-effective mean to keep large areas under 24/7 surveillance and to decrease the number of security personnel. Now more than ever, CCTV is becoming more popular in today’s working and social environments and it is being used in many locations such as governmental institutes, banks, private companies, airports, railway stations, military bases etc.

Biometric Access Control

Biometrics is highly advanced automated method for recognizing individuals based on a physiological or behavioural characteristic. After the preserve of the military and government, Biometrics technology has finally become commercially accessible and affordable. With misidentification rates of an incredible 1 in 1.2 million, Biometric technology offers significantly higher levels of security and precision identification. Solutions such as Iris Pattern Matching eliminate the need for ID cards or password systems, increasing security still further whilst reducing management time and costs

Vehicle Number Plate Recognition

Vehicle Number Plate Recognition Cameras provides a method for capturing vehicle registration information that is both user-friendly and reliable. As well as number plates, VNPR Cameras can also take images of vehicles and vehicle drivers. These images are then stored in a special database, allowing detailed vehicle information to be retrieved at the touch of a button.
